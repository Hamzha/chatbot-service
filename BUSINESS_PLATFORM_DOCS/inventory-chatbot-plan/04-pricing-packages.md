# Pricing and Packages

## Recommended Package Structure

## Starter

- 1 brand/tenant
- Website chatbot only
- CSV inventory upload
- Basic analytics
- Email support

Suggested price: USD 49 to 99 per month

## Growth

- Website + WhatsApp
- Scheduled inventory sync
- Human handoff
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support

Suggested price: USD 299 to 999 per month

## Enterprise

- Custom integrations (ERP/POS/CRM)
- SSO and advanced security
- SLA and dedicated support
- Custom deployment options

Suggested price: USD 2,000+ per month

## Additional Revenue Streams

- One-time onboarding/setup fee
- Paid integration packs
- Managed AI tuning service
- Conversation automation consulting

## Packaging Strategy

- Keep entry plan affordable for fast adoption.
- Lock core channel value in Growth plan.
- Position Enterprise around compliance, integration depth, and support guarantees.
