# Product Vision

## Goal

Build a platform businesses can use to:

- Upload inventory and stock data
- Train an AI assistant on their catalog and policies
- Answer customer questions automatically
- Respond through website chat and WhatsApp

## Ideal Customers

- Small and medium retail businesses
- Distributors and wholesalers
- D2C brands with frequent product and stock queries

## Problems Solved

- Repetitive support questions about product availability
- Slow response times during peak hours
- Inconsistent answers from manual agents
- Lost sales when customers do not get instant responses

## Core Value Proposition

"Connect your inventory once, and let AI answer customers accurately on your website and WhatsApp 24/7."

## Must-Have Product Capabilities

- Secure inventory data ingestion
- Real-time or near-real-time stock sync
- Retrieval-augmented Q&A for accurate answers
- Channel delivery (WhatsApp + website widget)
- Conversation analytics and lead capture
